Mac and Cheese Pizza

Description

Do you like macaroni and cheese? Do you like pizza? Then what better meal to make than combining them both to make a cheesy delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • ¾ cup cavatappi (corkscrew macaroni)
  • ⅔ (16 ounce) jar cheese sauce (such as Ragu® Double Cheddar), divided, or as needed
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Place pizza crust on a baking sheet.
  2. Bring a small pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook cavatappi in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until cooked through but firm to the bite, 10 to 11 minutes. Drain and return pasta to the pot.
  3. Stir 1/2 the cheese sauce and butter into the cavatappi pot over medium heat until butter is melted and pasta-cheese mixture is combined. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Spread a thin layer of cheese sauce over the pizza crust. Sprinkle shredded Cheddar cheese over the cheese sauce onto the crust. Pour pasta-cheese mixture over the Cheddar cheese, and top with another 1/2 the cheese sauce.
  5. Bake pizza in the preheated oven until golden and bubbling, 8 to 10 minutes. Let rest for 2 minutes before slicing.
